Telangana Dishes

Hyderabadi Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 500g Basmati rice
  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 2 Onions (sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • Yogurt, spices, and herbs

Recipe:

1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices for at least 1 hour.
2. Fry onions until golden brown.
3. Layer marinated chicken and partially cooked rice in a pot.
4. Cook on low heat for about 30-40 minutes.

Gongura Pachadi

Ingredients:

  • 200g Gongura leaves
  • 2-3 Green chilies
  • 1 tsp Tamarind paste
  • Salt, mustard seeds

Recipe:

1. Sauté Gongura leaves and green chilies.
2. Blend with tamarind paste and salt.
3. Temper with mustard seeds.

Ragi Mudde

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Ragi flour
  • Water
  • Salt

Recipe:

1. Boil water and add salt.
2. Gradually add Ragi flour, stirring to avoid lumps.
3. Cook until it forms a thick dough. Shape into balls.
